Salmon Sheet Pan

Description

Meat and potatoes are a classic dish. Never has the combination been a healthy or quick as this sheet pan recipe!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Baby gold potatoes
  • Green beans
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Heat oven
  2. Add potatoes and green beans to bowl with spices
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Lay vegetables on sheet
  5. Cover with salmon and bake

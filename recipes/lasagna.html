Lasagna

Description

Meat, chesse, sauce and noodles can combine to make many different wonderful recipes. However none quite compare to that of lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses:
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest

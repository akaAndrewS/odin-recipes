Buffalo Chicken Sandwich

Description

Chicken sandwiches are the hot item right now. Make this popular dish at home with your own heat!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (boneless)
  • Sandwhich buns
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Vegetables:
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Onion

Steps

  1. Put chicken in slow cooker
  2. Add buffalo sauce
  3. Cook 6 hours
  4. Shred chicken
  5. Place chicken on bun with vegetables
